About Paul James

This site is my personal homepage, a place for the rants and ravings of a code junkie, and a home for my various projects, experiments, odds and ends.

I have been designing and developing Web pages since early 1998, I am a self-taught Web designer and programmer relying heavily on information from the many sources of the Internet and the World Wide Web.

I believe heavily in developing Web applications using the KISS principles of "less is more" and am a proponent of the REST architectural style.
